What Types of Roofing Materials Do You Offer?

We offer a wide range of high-quality roofing materials to meet the needs of homes and businesses. Our services include asphalt shingles for a durable and cost-effective option,metal roofing for long-lasting protection against harsh weather, cedar shakes for a natural and classic look, flat roofing for low-slope or commercial applications, and Euroshield rubber roofing for a resilient synthetic alternative. No matter the material, our team ensures expert installation and reliable results.

How Do I Know If My Roof Needs Repair?

You may need a roof repair if you notice signs of damage or wear. Common indicators include missing, cracked, or curling shingles, leaks or water stains inside your home, damaged flashing, or granules from shingles collecting in gutters. Other warning signs can be sagging areas, moss or algae growth, or excessive age—most roofs last 20–30 years depending on the material. If you’re unsure, our experts at SB7 Roofing can inspect your roof and provide a professional assessment to prevent small issues from becoming costly problems.

How Can I Maintain My Roof After Installation?

Proper maintenance helps extend the life of your roof and prevents costly repairs. Regularly inspect your roof for damaged or missing shingles, check for leaks or water stains, and keep gutters and downspouts clear of debris. Trim overhanging branches that could damage your roof during storms, and remove moss or algae buildup when needed. It’s also a good idea to schedule a professional roof inspection every few years to catch issues early. Following these steps ensures your roof stays strong and reliable for years to come.

Are Your Roofing Services Covered by Warranty?

Yes — when you choose SB7 Roofing, many of the roofing materials we install come with strong manufacturer warranties, and the company also backs its work with a workmanship guarantee. For example, asphalt shingles installed through SB7 come with 30‑ to 50‑year product warranties on the materials, and each roofing job includes a 5‑year workmanship guarantee on the installation itself, giving you added peace of mind that both the product and the installation are protected.
